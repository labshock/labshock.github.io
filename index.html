About Labshock

Labshock

Hands-on OT/ICS Security Lab

Labshock is a practical OT/ICS cybersecurity lab designed for hands-on learning, testing, and research. It provides a ready-to-use environment for security professionals, researchers, and engineers to explore industrial protocols, attack scenarios, and defensive strategies.

The goal of Labshock is to bridge the gap between IT and OT security by offering a controlled environment where users can simulate real-world attacks, monitor network traffic, and develop effective security measures. Whether you're testing ICS visibility tools, refining SIEM integrations, or running red team exercises, Labshock provides the infrastructure to do it safely.

Labshock is built to support both individual learners and organizations looking to enhance their OT security capabilities. By focusing on accessibility and realism, it enables deeper understanding and practical skills that are essential for securing industrial environments.

Tidal Collector - OT SIEM Data Pipeline

  • Tidal Collector seamlessly integrates data flow into your OT SIEM.
  • Collect data from OT sources with minimal performance impact.
  • Normalize and aggregate data before forwarding to save costs.
  • Analyze data before sending it to SIEM, reducing noise.
  • Simple and fast web UI to check connected devices.
